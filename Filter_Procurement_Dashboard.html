🛠️ Inventory Formula & Ordering Rules

  • Lead Time (LT): Standardized to 0.5 Months (2 Weeks) representing local sourcing/vendor turnaround time.
  • Safety Stock (SS): Calculated as `AMD * SS_Factor` (rounded up). Buffer levels: 0.50 mo for Class A, 0.30 mo for Class B, 0.15 mo for Class C. Helps prevent emergency breakdowns.
  • Reorder Point (ROP): Calculated as `math.ceil(AMD * LT + SS)`. Rule: When the physical stock in the store room drops below this quantity, immediately place a replenishment order.
  • 1-Month / 3-Month Order Quantities: Calculated as `math.ceil(AMD * Cycle + SS)`. Rounded to full filter units. Represents ideal orders to minimize shipping costs and avoid surplus.
